Simplicity

Tired of overpriced meals at restaurant and of not knowing what goes into your plate? Passionnate about food and gastronomy for years, I cook simple, delicious meals with products sourced by myself, all hosted by me in the comfort of my living room.

At the price you decide ahead.

Omakase

Omakase is a Japanese phrase meaning "I'll leave it up to you".

I will be cooking ingredients of my choice based on your price and preferences.

The menu will include at least:

  • Mise-en-bouche
  • Starter
  • Meat / Fish / Vegetables

Your Choice

Everything is in your hands. You just have to choose the amount you want to spend on a dinner.

You are free to bring anything you would like to drink.

I can also offer wine pairing. Bear in mind that you should count that on top of your dinner price.

My Craft

Based on the price you decided, I will cook a full dinner based on products I source, and recipes I choose.

Since meals are based on products I source as locally as possible, they will vary depending on season and availability.

Your dinner

Everything is organized by me. All you have to do is to show up and enjoy your dinner.

No idea of a price for meals or for the wine, but having ideas about what you would like to have? No problem, mention it when booking. I will get back to you to provide you with an idea of what can be done.
